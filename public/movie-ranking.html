My Best Movies I've Watched by Lalaine

Here are the some of the movies that I think is the best!

CoCo

It has great animation. Very fun to watch and one of the best movies that makes my heart melt and eyes cry.

The Green Mile

This is one of the movie that changed my perspective in life. Such a heartbreaking story yet somehow gives a beautiful lesson about life.

My Neighbor Totoro

Very simple and cute storyline. Beautiful animation and such a great movie to watch when you're bored.
